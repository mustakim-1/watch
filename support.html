Frequently Asked Questions

All Dialcraft watches come with a 2-year international warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty includes free repairs for any issues with the movement, case, or dial. Water resistance and crystal damage are not covered under warranty. Please keep your purchase receipt as proof of warranty.

Orders within Bangladesh are delivered within 3-5 business days. International orders take 7-14 business days depending on the destination. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn watches in original condition with all packaging and documentation. Custom-engraved watches cannot be returned. Exchanges are accepted within 14 days. Contact our support team to initiate a return or exchange.

Avoid extreme temperatures, magnetic fields, and strong impacts. Clean with a soft cloth and mild soap solution. Have your watch serviced every 3-5 years by a professional. Water-resistant watches should be rinsed with fresh water after exposure to salt water.

Yes! We offer various customization options including engraving, strap changes, and dial modifications. Visit our customization page or contact us for more details. Custom orders typically take 2-3 weeks to complete.

We accept bKash, Nagad, credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery. All payments are processed securely through SSL encryption. For international orders, we also accept PayPal and bank transfers.
